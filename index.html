About us

CSS was established 2001 and is a student organization under the Chalmers Student Union of the Chalmers University of Technology. We aim to raise awareness about sustainability and encourage student and citizens engagement in our area. Our actions are through a variety of projects and we have over the years arranged events from food rescuing, clothes swap parties, social inclusion, recycling, waste picking in Gothenburg´s Archipelago, to lectures. We also helped Chalmers to divest from fossil fuels and calculated the carbon footprint for the different menus at Chalmers.
